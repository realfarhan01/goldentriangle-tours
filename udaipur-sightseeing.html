One-Day Udaipur Sightseeing Itinerary

8:00 AM - Sunrise at Lake Fatehsagar

Start your day with a peaceful sunrise at Lake Fatehsagar. Enjoy the calm waters, the chirping birds, and take some time for meditation.

9:30 AM - Visit the City Palace

Head to the magnificent City Palace, one of the largest royal complexes in India. Explore the various palaces, museums, and courtyards within the complex. Hire a private guide or use an audio guide to learn about the rich history.

12:30 PM - Lunch at a Local Thali Restaurant

Savor a traditional Udaipur vegetarian thali at a restaurant near Lake Pichola, such as Ambrai Restaurant or Upre Restaurant.

2:00 PM - Boat Ride on Lake Pichola

Take a boat ride on the serene Lake Pichola, enjoying views of the City Palace, Taj Lake Palace Hotel, Jagat Niwas Palace, and the picturesque ghats.

3:30 PM - Visit Doodh Talai Lake

Explore the charming Doodh Talai Lake, surrounded by bougainvillea and lush greenery. It's a peaceful spot to relax and take in the natural beauty.

4:30 PM - Sunset at Gangaur Ghat

Head to Gangaur Ghat to witness a stunning sunset over the lake. This is a popular spot for Hindu rituals and festivals.

6:00 PM - Explore the Old City

Wander through the vibrant old town, browsing the local markets and shops for traditional Rajasthani textiles, handicrafts, and souvenirs.

8:00 PM - Dinner at 1559 AD

End your day with a delectable dinner at 1559 AD, a restaurant near Lake Fatehsagar. Savor dishes like the renowned Lal Maas.
