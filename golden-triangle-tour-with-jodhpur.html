Golden Triangle Tour with Jodhpur

Here is your itinerary in detail, day by day

Embark on a mesmerizing journey through India's famed Golden Triangle, enriched by the regal allure of Jodhpur's blue cityscape. Discover a tapestry of history, culture, and architectural marvels starting from the royal palaces of Jodhpur to the vibrant cities of Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur.

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi
Day 2: Delhi Sightseeing
Day 3: Delhi to Agra
Day 4: Agra to Jaipur
Day 5: Jaipur
Day 1: Arrival in Delhi
  • Morning: Arrive in Delhi and check into your hotel. Freshen up and prepare for a day of sightseeing.
  • Afternoon: Start with a visit to Qutub Minar, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the tallest brick minaret in the world. Next, head to India Gate, a war memorial located in the heart of the city. Continue to the Lotus Temple, known for its unique flower-like architecture and serene ambiance.
  • Evening: Spend the evening at Connaught Place, a bustling commercial and financial hub in Delhi. Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant, sampling some of Delhi's famous street food or dining at a fine establishment.
Day 2: Delhi Sightseeing
  • Morning: Visit Red Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of India's rich history. Then, visit Jama Masjid, one of the largest mosques in India.
  • Afternoon: Explore Chandni Chowk, a bustling market area with narrow winding lanes, shops, and eateries. Visit Raj Ghat, the memorial of Mahatma Gandhi.
  • Evening: Visit Humayun's Tomb, another UNESCO World Heritage Site. Drive past the Presidential House and the Parliament House.
Day 3: Delhi to Agra
  • Morning: After breakfast, drive to Agra (approximately 4 hours). Upon arrival, check into your hotel and take some time to relax.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Spend time exploring this iconic monument and learning about its history. Afterwards, visit Agra Fort, another UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its impressive architecture and rich history.
  • Evening: Explore the local markets of Agra, where you can shop for souvenirs and enjoy the local cuisine.
Day 4: Agra to Jaipur
  • Morning: After breakfast, drive to Jaipur, stopping en route at Fatehpur Sikri (approximately 5 hours total). Fatehpur Sikri is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and was once the capital of the Mughal Empire. Explore the well-preserved palaces and courtyards.
  • Afternoon: Arrive in Jaipur and check into your hotel. Visit the City Palace, a stunning complex that includes the Chandra Mahal and Mubarak Mahal palaces, and the Jantar Mantar, an astronomical observatory and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Evening: Explore Jaipur's vibrant markets, such as Johari Bazaar and Bapu Bazaar. Enjoy a dinner featuring traditional Rajasthani cuisine.
Day 5: Jaipur
  • Morning: Visit Amber Fort, located on a hilltop just outside the city. Enjoy an elephant ride or a jeep ride up to the fort. Explore its beautiful courtyards, halls, and gardens. On the way back to the city, stop by Hawa Mahal, also known as the Palace of Winds, and Jal Mahal, a palace situated in the middle of the Man Sagar Lake.
  • Afternoon: Visit Albert Hall Museum, which houses a rich collection of artifacts, and the Birla Temple, known for its stunning marble architecture.
  • Evening: Enjoy a cultural evening with Rajasthani folk music and dance performances.
Day 6: Jaipur to Jodhpur
Day 7: Jodhpur
Day 8: Jodhpur to Delhi
Day 6: Jaipur to Jodhpur
  • Morning: After breakfast, drive to Jodhpur (approximately 6 hours). Check into your hotel upon arrival.
  • Afternoon: Visit the imposing Mehrangarh Fort, one of the largest forts in India, offering panoramic views of the city. Explore its museum which houses an extensive collection of artifacts.
  • Evening: Stroll through the vibrant local markets of Jodhpur, such as the Clock Tower Market, and enjoy the local cuisine.
Day 7: Jodhpur
  • Morning: Visit Jaswant Thada, a stunning marble cenotaph built in memory of Maharaja Jaswant Singh II. Then, explore the beautiful Umaid Bhawan Palace, one of the world's largest private residences, part of which is now a museum.
  • Afternoon: Take a tour of the Mandore Gardens, home to an array of temples, memorials, and high rock terraces.
  • Evening: Enjoy a relaxing evening exploring the local bazaars or relaxing at your hotel.
Day 8: Jodhpur to Delhi
  • Morning: After breakfast, you can either take a flight or drive back to Delhi (driving takes approximately 10 hours, so a flight is recommended).
  • Afternoon: Arrive in Delhi and check into your hotel or prepare for your departure.
  • Evening: Depart for your onward journey, concluding your Golden Triangle Tour with Jodhpur.

GoldenTriangle Tours specializes in creating unforgettable travel experiences through India's most iconic destinations: Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur. With a commitment to quality, personalized service, and in-depth cultural insights, we ensure every journey is seamless and enriching.
